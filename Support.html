Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adjust the app settings? Navigate to the 'Settings' icon on your GoldenAssist app dashboard. Here you can adjust various preferences according to your needs.
1. What is GoldenAssist? - Answer: GoldenAssist is a user-friendly smartphone application designed to simplify the digital experience for the elderly. With easy-to-use features, it aids in everyday tasks, reminders, and essential functionalities, ensuring that technology remains approachable and convenient.
2. How do I install GoldenAssist on my smartphone? - Answer: Visit your phone's app store, search for "GoldenAssist," and click on the "Install" button. Once downloaded, open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to set it up.
3. Is GoldenAssist compatible with all smartphones? - Answer: GoldenAssist is designed to work with most modern smartphones. We currently support Android and iOS platforms. Ensure your phone's operating system is updated for the best experience.
4. How do I customize notifications and reminders? - Answer: Go to the 'Reminders' section within the app. Here, you can set new reminders or adjust existing ones according to your needs. For notifications, visit 'Settings' and select 'Notification Preferences'.
5. I'm having trouble hearing the notifications. How can I increase the volume? - Answer: Within the GoldenAssist app, navigate to 'Settings' and select 'Sound Preferences'. Here, you can adjust the volume and choose a louder notification tone if needed.
6. Can I use GoldenAssist in a language other than English? - Answer: Yes! GoldenAssist is multilingual. Visit the 'Settings' and select 'Language Preferences' to choose from our available language options.
7. Is my personal information safe with GoldenAssist? - Answer: Absolutely. Protecting your privacy is our top priority. GoldenAssist uses advanced encryption techniques to keep your data secure. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information.
8. How can I provide feedback or suggest new features? - Answer: We value your input! Within the app, navigate to the 'Feedback' section to share your thoughts, experiences, or suggest improvements.
9. What do I do if the app crashes or stops working? - Answer: First, try restarting your device. If the issue persists, you can uninstall and reinstall GoldenAssist from your app store. If you continue to face problems, please contact our support team.
10. Are there any costs associated with using GoldenAssist? - Answer: GoldenAssist offers a free basic version, but we also have a premium version with additional features available for a nominal fee. All pricing details are available within the app.
