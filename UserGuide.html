GoldenAssist User Guide

Welcome to the user guide for GoldenAssist! Whether you're a tech guru or a beginner, we've made sure this guide will help you get the most out of our app.

Getting Started

  1. Download GoldenAssist from your preferred app store.
  2. Open the app and follow the on-screen prompts to set up your account.
  3. Allow necessary permissions for the best experience.

Contact Management

Under the "Contacts" tab, you can easily add, edit, or remove contacts. Simply tap on a contact to call or message them directly from the app.

Setting Reminders

Navigate to the "Reminders" section:

  1. Click on the "+" icon.
  2. Fill in the task details and set a time.
  3. Save, and the app will remind you when it's time.
Health Tracking

If your device supports health tracking, connect it to GoldenAssist from the "Health" tab and view your health metrics in a simple layout.

Getting Support

If you face any challenges, click on the "Support" tab and reach out to our dedicated team. We're here to help!
